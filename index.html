Currently Available

Playable today.

The core of Card Quest is live and ready — collect, progress, build, and battle.

🎁
Quest Packs

Open quest packs.

Complete daily and quest objectives to earn packs, then tear them open in a cinematic reveal. Every pull adds new cards to your collection and pushes your decks a little further.

🃏
Card Collection

Your bag, your book, your masterpiece.

Open animated packs and gather hundreds of cards across heroes and rarities. Browse everything in a fluid grid, track set completion in the Book, and chase the elusive Radiant variant — a roughly 2% shimmer that turns any card into a centerpiece.

✨
Progression System

Grow stronger with every match.

Level up as you play and ascend your favorite cards to climb their power curve, unlock new abilities, and celebrate every upgrade with a dedicated animation. Turn a common pull into a champion worth building around.

🧩
Deck Building

Craft a deck that fights the way you do.

Drag, drop, and validate your lineup in real time. Build around quickstrike tempo, raw power leads, or balanced control — the builder enforces the rules so you can focus on the strategy.

⚔️
Core Gameplay Loop

Battle, earn, open, repeat.

Duel in fast, strategic battles, complete fresh daily quests, and turn your rewards into new packs and stronger cards. It's the satisfying loop that keeps every session moving forward.

In Development

Coming soon.

Features in active development — taking Card Quest from a game into a social economy.

🤝
Player-to-Player Trading In dev

Trade directly with other players.

Swap cards face-to-face in secure, peer-to-peer trades. Complete a collection, hand a friend the card they've been hunting, and make every duplicate count.

💱
Live Marketplace In dev

A player-run economy that breathes.

List cards for sale, browse a constantly refreshing marketplace, and let supply and demand find their own price. Find a bargain or set the market — the economy is yours to shape.

🐉
Cooperative Raid Events In dev

Team up against raid bosses.

Take on towering co-op encounters and earn exclusive Raid packs with guaranteed high-power cards. Raid loot lands straight in your mailbox, ready to open whenever you want to celebrate the win.

🏆
Competitive Leaderboards In dev

Climb the ranks, prove your deck.

Ranked PvP with skill-based matchmaking and a global leaderboard. Every win pushes your rating higher — every loss is clamped to your earned tier floor, so the climb always feels fair.

🗓️
Seasonal Content Updates In dev

The world keeps evolving.

Recurring seasons bring rotating challenges, themed rewards, and fresh goals to chase. There's always a new reason to come back and a new peak to reach.
